We are a search engine of magnet link that can be used by people to access content using Bittorrent protocol.

Our goal is to provide a full featured search engine that respects your private life, and use modern technologies. Most of the information that are stored on this application are NEVER sent to the server (such as historic or configuration). The data generated by your navigation such as rating or statistics are kept under impersonnal form (e.g: we don't store which magnet you have rated).

We are not responsible of action relied to the use of this tool (including exploding cats in micro-wave). Note: sharing content that is raped by the right-holders is some kind of illegal (yup, you can be fined and go to prison for that), prefer sharing content in public domain, or under Libre licenses.
